Auction (Java)

Simulates an auctioning system through 4 components (Bank, Agents, Auction Central, and Auction Houses); capable of operating over a network. The Bank keeps track of accounts and makes adjustments to balances based on bidding activity, i.e. When a bid is placed a hold for the amount is placed and when an auction is successful the amount is withdrawn.

Agents first register for bank accounts, then register with Auction Central, receiving connections to Auction Houses. Agents are then capable of placing bids and winning auctions.
